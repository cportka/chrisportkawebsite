About

Chris Portka is a songwriter and multi-instrumentalist based in Berkeley, California. After spending five years in Voice Movement Therapy to overcome a lifelong fear of singing, he began releasing records in 2020 — nine albums spanning folk, psych, experimental rock, and ambient.

His 2025 record The Album Everyone Wants, co-produced with Jasper Leach at New York’s Sear Sound and Oakland’s Brothers Recording, draws on the American songbook’s stranger corners: Syd Barrett, Skip Spence, George Jones, and his own quiet, observational writing.

“A David Berman for the Bay.”

— Obscure Sound
