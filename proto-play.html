Proto Play

Records and side projects from before the Chris Portka era

Before the records released under my own name there was a decade of music made under other names. Two early projects in particular — Rhiao and Beardwail — formed the proto-version of what eventually became this site. Most of these are still up on Bandcamp, some are buried on SoundCloud and YouTube, a few are gone.

Rhiao

Solo project, Oakland, 2014–2015. Pop, experimental, ambient, impressionist, primitive. Two albums on Bandcamp, both name-your-price.

  1. what rhymes with now?

    January 2014

    Single 11:28 track. Left and right keyboard by nicholas r. chen.

  2. first breathe, best breathe

    January 2015

    Eight tracks. First take, best take. Sunshine girl features Beardwail.

  3. Beardwail & Rhiao — All-Else

    October 2014

    YouTube only.

Your Entire Arm Is Red

Oakland, 2014. Drone folk, experimental, noise, and (per the Bandcamp tag) “laughable.” One release.

  1. out-takes

    September 2014

    Single track, the renaissance man of today. Priced at $1,000.

Beardwail

Tom Meagher’s long-running San Francisco project. Creative collaborator across decades — features Rhiao on Out of Time and Sassaphrasing, dedicated Deep Fried Killer Whale to me, and plays lead guitar across The Album Everyone Wants.
