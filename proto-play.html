Proto Play

Records and side projects from before the Chris Portka era

Before the records released under the name my ever loving parents gave me, were decades of obscure music made under other names, some released and some still unreleased. Two early projects, Rhiao (aka me) and Beardwail (aka Tom Meagher) are full of playful, art-y, and eclectic music. Most is still up on Bandcamp, some are buried on SoundCloud and YouTube, a few are gone to the sands of time, and even more have never been released.

Rhiao

Solo project, Oakland, 2014–2015. Pop, experimental, ambient, impressionist, primitive. Two albums on Bandcamp.

  1. what rhymes with now?

    January 2014

    Ambient impressionism with wafting guitars and pianos. Features some piano work by Nicholas R. Chen.

  2. first breathe, best breathe

    January 2015

    Eight suffocatingly mean tracks with glimpses of the light. First take, best take. Sunshine girl features Beardwail.

  3. Beardwail & Rhiao — All-Else

    October 2014

Beardwail

A limited sampling of the legend Tom Meagher’s musical output. Creative collaborator across decades — features Rhiao on Out of Time and Sassaphrasing, even a song “Deep Fried Killer Whale” about someone you might know. Tom also plays lead guitar across The Album Everyone Wants.
